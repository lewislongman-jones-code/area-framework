What separates the 5%?

MIT research across hundreds of organisations is unambiguous: the difference between those capturing real value from AI and those stuck in pilot mode is not the sophistication of the model. It is the quality of the foundations beneath it — data, decision clarity, workflow design, and organisational alignment.

The Forbes Effect is real. Board pressure is high, headlines suggest everyone is moving fast, and the temptation is to act before the foundations are ready. But agentic AI amplifies whatever it finds — great signals become great customer decisions, while gaps in data, misaligned metrics, and unclear processes get automated at scale before anyone notices.

"The organisations that win won't have the best models. They'll have the best signals, the clearest decision frameworks, and the alignment to act on both — at speed."

These aren't hypothetical risks — they show up in every deployment that stalls. The organisations in the 5% who deliver measurable ROI addressed them early, deliberately, and before they scaled. The assessment that follows helps you understand which of these are live issues for your organisation right now.

"When the foundations are right, AI doesn't just work — it compounds. Every signal gets sharper, every decision gets better, and every customer experience gets more relevant."
